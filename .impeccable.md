## Design Context

### Users
Restaurant patrons, food enthusiasts, and prospective diners looking for a refined dining experience. They want to view the menu, learn about the chef, and easily make a reservation.

### Brand Personality
Refined, Minimalist, Modern, Elegant.

### Aesthetic Direction
Clean, modern design with a monochromatic color scheme, ample white space, and elegant typography. Subtle hover animations and transitions. No cluttered layouts. 

### Design Principles
1. **Monochromatic & Minimal:** Stick to a single primary color scale (often grays/blacks with one subtle accent) to let the food imagery shine.
2. **Whitespace is Luxury:** Generous padding and margins to create a high-end feel.
3. **Elegant Typography:** Use a distinctive display font paired with a highly readable body font.
4. **Smooth Interactions:** Implement smooth scrolling and subtle, non-intrusive hover animations.
5. **Accessible & Functional:** High contrast, ARIA labels, keyboard navigation, and clear calls to action (like reservations).
